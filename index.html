How we work

From first call to a system you own.

A clear path with a working version in your hands fast, then a focused window to make it fit like a glove. No mystery, and you own everything at the end.

01
Step one

AI audit

We sit with how your business runs, find where time and money leak, and map the highest-leverage system to build first. You leave with a roadmap either way. If it is a fit, that roadmap becomes a fixed-scope proposal.

02

Honest fit

We are not for everyone. Here is who we are for.

We do our best work with established businesses ready to change how the day to day runs. If that is you, let's talk. If it is not, we will tell you straight and point you somewhere better.

A fit if

This is the size where custom AI tooling pays for itself the fastest. It is simply where we move the needle the most.

  • You are doing $1M or more a year, or you have 5 or more employees
  • You run an established business, not a side project
  • You are ready to change how the work actually gets done

Not a fit if

No hard feelings. These are just not places we can do our best work, and we would rather say so than waste your time.

  • Hobbyists and side projects
  • Just curious and kicking the tires
  • Legal, banking, or major medical (compliance heavy, outside our lane)
  • Franchises
