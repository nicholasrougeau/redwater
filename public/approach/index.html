How we work

From first call to a system you own.

The full picture of a Redwater engagement, start to finish. What happens at each stage, when you pay, and why you own everything at the end. No mystery, no surprises.

01
Step one

AI audit

A deep working session on how your business runs. We map where time and money leak and pick the highest-leverage system to build first. You leave with a roadmap either way. If it is a fit, that roadmap becomes a fixed-scope proposal.

02
Step two

Onboarding

Once you are in, we kick off. A detailed brief, access to what we need, and a locked scope so the build starts on rails. Half on signing gets the work moving.

03
Step three

Version one delivery

We build in tight loops and ship a working v1 you can actually use, in your name. You see real software early, not slides. The second half is due when v1 goes live.

04
Step four

90-day iteration

A focused window to polish and refine the live system until it fits. Refinement, not feature creep, then it is fully yours to run.

Who we do our best work with.

  • You are doing $1M or more a year, or you have 5 or more employees
